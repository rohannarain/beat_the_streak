Beat the Streak with ML

What is this?

This project involves using several machine learning models (Random Forests, AdaBoost, and Logistic Regression specifically) to play Major League Baseball's Beat The Streak contest. The idea is simple: every day, you decide which player you think is most likely to get a hit on that given day, and you enter that pick into the app. The goal is to get a streak going--if you pick 56 players in a row correctly, you'll win a lot of money. I'll have a blog post written about how I did this soon.

What do I do with the table on the home page?

The table displays the top 10 most likely players to get a hit today, based on the probabilities outputted by the Random Forests algorithm. It's up to you to use whatever context you feel is necessary (opposing team, ballpark, etc.) in addition to this table to make sure your picks are intelligent and informed.
